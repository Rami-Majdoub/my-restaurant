## About my-restaurant

A restaurant management app.</br>
Running a restaurant can be exhausting and nerve-racking.</br>
This is an app to help restaurants organize and keep track of the customer's orders.</br>

* Menu is the list of products that can be served. can be customized by creating, editing, and deleting their own products.
* Tables are a representation of the actual tables. shows the list of unpaid bills associated with each table.
* Bills are the customer orders.
